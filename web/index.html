First pass at aggregation and analysis.

In total we found 677 URLs in the opinons. The first URL appeared in 1996 and the latest appeared on July 3, 2014 (where our dataset ended).

Of the 677 URLs we found, 315, or 47% are no longer pointing to the material they cite (link/reference rot)

We found that web archivsts have been busy. Of the 677 URLs, 546, or 81% have been archived by a web archiving service

Even more important, 230 of our 315 rotten URLs, or 73%, have an archive available on the Web.
